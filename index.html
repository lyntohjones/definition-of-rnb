GRAMN.

London-based R&B Collective

GRAMN. began with the bold sound of a young woman from Hackney, London named Aux, who enjoyed creating unapologetically honest music. The collective was formed when Aux met producers and multi-instrumentalists James Lowe and Johnny Tomlinson, creating a trio that blends groove-laden R&B, hip-hop lyricism, and experimental production.

Their sound is a unique combination of tightly wound R&B with Aux's gifted vocals and flamboyant persona, quickly carving a unique path with the sole aim of making authentic art. Influenced by artists like Kendrick Lamar, Missy Elliott, and Lauryn Hill, GRAMN. explores both personal and global issues through their music.
