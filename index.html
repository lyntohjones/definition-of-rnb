GRAMN.

London-based R&B Collective

GRAMN. began with the bold sound of a young woman from Hackney, London named Aux, who enjoyed creating unapologetically honest music. The collective was formed when Aux met producers and multi-instrumentalists James Lowe and Johnny Tomlinson, creating a trio that blends groove-laden R&B, hip-hop lyricism, and experimental production.

Their sound is a unique combination of tightly wound R&B with Aux's gifted vocals and flamboyant persona, quickly carving a unique path with the sole aim of making authentic art. Influenced by artists like Kendrick Lamar, Missy Elliott, and Lauryn Hill, GRAMN. explores both personal and global issues through their music.

About The Definition of R&B

Welcome to The Definition of R&B, your premier source for discovering the future of rhythm and blues. We're passionate about showcasing talented upcoming artists and bringing you the latest news, interviews, and sounds from the R&B world.

Inspired by the vibes on our Instagram page, this platform aims to dive deeper into the music and the stories behind it. We celebrate the evolution of R&B from its soulful roots to its innovative present, highlighting artists who push boundaries while honoring tradition.

Join our community and explore the evolving landscape of R&B with us, from established icons to the rising stars crafting tomorrow's classics.
