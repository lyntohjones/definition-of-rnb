Rising R&B Artists to Watch

Discover the fresh sounds shaping the future of R&B from the UK, Toronto, and the US.

From the UK

Mya K

South London, UK (originally from Nigeria)

Mya K is known for her sultry R&B vocals, recently pivoting to incorporate liquid DnB-inspired production. A Brit School graduate, she was nominated for 'Fresher of the Year' at the State of RnB Awards. Her music often explores vulnerability and storytelling.

Read More
Listen To:
  • Search for her single "Distraction" (released early 2024/late 2023) or her work on Spotify, Apple Music, Bandcamp.

Hendrix Harris

London, UK

A versatile singer, rapper, composer, and producer, Hendrix Harris blends R&B, soul, and hip-hop with jazz sensibilities. His work is often reflective, covering themes of self, society, relationships, and growth.

Read More
Listen To:
  • His album "Awakening" and singles like "The Surge" and "Incentive" (released 2024) can be found on major streaming platforms and YouTube.

Idris Jones

UK

Idris Jones fuses electronic R&B with UK rap, drawing inspiration from 2000s hip-hop, grime, and R&B. He is noted for his storytelling, weaving narratives through emotional and sometimes witty lyrics.

Read More
Listen To:
  • His single "I Need You" (released January 2024) is available on streaming platforms.

Diedrick

Derby-born, London-based, UK

Diedrick is recognized as a promising emerging R&B artist in the UK, known for modern takes on throwback sounds. His music is often described as soulful and nostalgic.

Read More
Listen To:
  • His single "Don't Lose Your Crown" ft. AZA (released August 2023) can be found on major streaming services.

FLISS

Chester-born, Manchester-based, UK

FLISS combines R&B, hazy soul, and pop into hypnotic soundscapes. Her music has shown growth, with recent work incorporating Latin influences.

Read More
Listen To:
  • Her single "4LYF" (released June 2023) and debut EP "Situations" are available on platforms like Spotify.

Ayda Rose (Provisional)

Likely UK-based (not explicitly stated, but featured on UK-centric music blog DMY).

Ayda Rose creates slick R&B with buttery vocals, often focusing on themes of heartbreak and raw emotion. Her sound is described as "soulful with a modern twist."

Read More
Listen To:
  • Her single "Damn" (released February 2024) and "Psychic" (2023) are on Spotify.

Toronto Spotlight

Nonso Amadi

Nigerian-Canadian, Toronto-based

An Afropop and R&B artist, Nonso Amadi is known for his versatile vocals and has dabbled in electronic music. His lyrics often reflect on his journey and success.

Read More
Listen To:
  • Check out his collaboration "Deya" with Kito (2024) and "Babyboo" with Sylo (2024) on YouTube and other platforms.

Sylo

Toronto, Canada

A rising R&B star, Sylo is noted for his distinct voice and cadence, often collaborating with other Toronto artists. His music features global inspirations in its production.

Read More
Listen To:
  • His track "Babyboo" featuring Nonso Amadi (released July 2024) and his upcoming project "Dreamt That I Was" are good starting points. Search on YouTube and streaming services.

Quarterback Baby

Toronto, Canada

A pop singer with R&B and dance influences, Quarterback Baby is known for slick anthems with earworm hooks and emotional depth, channeling high-energy pop vibes.

Read More
Listen To:
  • Her single "Cheerleader" (released July 2024) from the upcoming album "Hypersexual Heartbreak" can be found on YouTube.

Hailing from the US

Chenayder

Orlando, Florida, US

A 17-year-old singer blending indie-pop with '50s/'60s pop harmonies and modern breakbeats. Her music is described as "vintage and sorrowful," with lyrical wisdom beyond her years. She has received a co-sign from Tyler, the Creator.

Read More
Listen To:
  • Her debut EP "Blue Oblivion" (2023) and songs like "Fall," "Strawberry Perfume," and "For One Last Time" are available on streaming platforms.

4batz

Dallas, Texas, US

4batz is an R&B singer known for his distinctive pitched-up melodies and unique presentation (often seen in a ski mask). He has gained rapid online traction and co-signs from major artists like SZA, Drake, and Kanye West.

Read More
Listen To:
  • Look for his "From The Block" performance and his "act" series of singles (e.g., "act iii: on god?") on YouTube and streaming services.

Kaicrewsade

Chicago, Illinois, US

A rapper with a strong R&B and jazz influence in his music, often compared to artists like Smino and Erykah Badu. He's known for agile raps packed with personality and a focus on community.

Read More
Listen To:
  • His single "Chickenscratch!" is a good example of his style. Search for his music on various platforms.

Anycia (Provisional)

Atlanta, Georgia, US

While primarily a rapper, Anycia expresses a strong love for R&B and cites R&B vocalists as significant influences. Her music features clever, memorable bars and consistent energy.
